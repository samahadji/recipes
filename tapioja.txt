Ingredients:
 - 2 cups soja beverage
 - 1/2 cup tapioca
 - 12 cubes of sugar
 - 1 microwave oven

Directions:
 1. Put everything together in one bowl
 
 2. Set the microwave to 400W
 
 3. Put the bowl in the microwave et set the cooking time to 6 minutes.

 4. After six minutes, take it out and enjoy, optionally with ground nuts!
